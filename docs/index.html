New Color Options

Kirby wouldn't gain the ability to change his color in game for many years after Kirby's Adventure... until now! The randomizer offers a selection of different color palettes to choose from, all across the rainbow! If that doesn't sound random enough for you, don't fret, you can a color palette chosen for you at random as well. For those of you who are truly crazy, there's also the ability to have a completely random set of colors chosen for Kirby. This often looks hideous, but hey, it was easy to add.
